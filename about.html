What's this website I'm on?

This platform aims to capture and explore the various styles seen on the streets of London. The founders, two international students at Queen Mary, were thrilled by the diverse mix of people living in London. However, everyone in the city is always seems to be a in rush and might miss out on the more eccentric culture of this city - expressed through fashion. To create an interactive space for both the creatives and observes, this website welcomes digital submissions!

Guidelines for submissions;

♡The pictures should be taken in London.

♡The person in the photo needs to have given permission should their face be included. If this is not the case, the Cliché Club would be obliged to take the photo down immediately if informed, and will not associate itself or protect the submitter should any legal action be undertaken by the person being photographed.

♡No photographs of minors (under 18).

♡No photographs portraying illegal activities.

♡Pictures must be taken in London.
